    About Me

    Hi! My name is Shane Bussmann. I have a PhD in astronomy from the University of Arizona in 2010. I completed a post-doc at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics in 2013, and just finished up another post-doc at Cornell University. I am currently a Fellow in the Insight Health Data Science program, because I am actively seeking to make a career change into data science. In my research as an astronomer, I learned about how stars form in distant galaxies by studying gravitationally lensed galaxies.
